UNDERSTAND THE MISSION BEFORE YOU BEGIN

You are about to enter a simulated digital environment designed to test how people respond to suspicious prompts, conflicting cues, and pressure-based decision making.

Across three rounds, you will face a series of six scenarios. Each one will present a digital message, alert, or prompt that requires you to make a decision.

Your available actions are:

  • PROCEED - engage with the prompt or continue the action
  • CANCEL - dismiss or avoid the action
  • REPORT - flag the content as suspicious
  • INVESTIGATE - inspect further before deciding

Your score will change depending on your choices. Some scenarios may look harmless, while others will use urgency, pressure, or familiarity to influence your judgement.

Respond as naturally as possible. The goal is not perfection. The goal is to observe how decisions change under pressure.
