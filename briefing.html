UNDERSTAND THE GAME BEFORE YOU BEGIN

You are about to enter a gamified environment designed to test your responses to various prompts.

Across three rounds, you will face a series of six short scenarios. Each one will present a digital message, alert or prompt that requires you to make a decision.

Your available actions are:

  • PROCEED - engage with the prompt or continue the action
  • CANCEL - dismiss or avoid the action
  • REPORT - flag the content as suspicious
  • INVESTIGATE - inspect further before deciding

Your score will depend on your choices.

Respond as naturally as possible.
