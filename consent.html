This study complies with the University of Gloucestershire’s Research Ethical Policy and the British Psychological Society Code of Human Research Ethics (2021).

Cortex Quest is a gamified cyber-security simulation designed to collect anonymous behavioural data, including user choices and response times. No personal or sensitive data will be collected.

Participation is entirely voluntary. By continuing, you confirm that you understand the nature of the study and consent to take part. You may withdraw at any time before completing the simulation.

All data will be stored securely, used solely for academic research purposes, and disposed of securely after the project is complete. The simulation does not involve deception or known significant risk.
