Thank you for taking part in Cortex Quest.

This simulation was designed as part of a dissertation project exploring how users respond to suspicious digital prompts under increasing levels of pressure, urgency, authority, and consequence-based framing.

Your responses have been recorded anonymously and will be used for academic analysis only. No personal or sensitive data has been collected through this simulation.

The purpose of the study is to examine how behavioural and cognitive factors may influence cyber-security decision-making, and whether gamified simulation can help reveal patterns of vulnerability and resilience under pressure.
